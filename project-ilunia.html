Asymmetric Co-op Puzzle Game

An asymmetric co-op puzzle game where one player must solve intricate puzzles in-game based on instructions from their partner, who has a different set of information. The goal is to join a mysterious cult.

This is the debut project from our studio, TigerOne Studios. As the Lead Programmer, I am responsible for the core technical architecture and team leadership.

Key Learnings & Features:

  • Teamwork & Leadership
  • Project & Time Management
  • Co-op Networking (Asymmetric Gameplay)
  • System Architecture Design
