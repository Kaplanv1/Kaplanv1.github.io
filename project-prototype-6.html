Project Information

Data-Driven Cooking Game

A time-management cooking/puzzle game where the player acts as a witch. The core mechanic involves reading recipes, gathering the correct ingredients, processing them through the right steps, and brewing them in a cauldron, similar to a restaurant management game.

Key Learnings & Features:

  • Inventory System
  • Data Assets & Data Tables
  • Data-Driven Gameplay Design
